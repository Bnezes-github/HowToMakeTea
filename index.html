How to make Tea

At the very least, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help your teeth and your heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Non-dairy (optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (optional)

Preparation

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and boil only it once to keep the oxugen level up. Oxigen in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug (always a big mug).
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs to unlock all its flavours, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its thing. This is a perfect time to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about holidays.
  5. If you want, throw in some milk or sugar or honey or nothing else at all.
  6. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon against the side of the mug. Not too much or you'll make it bitter.
  7. Enjoy!
